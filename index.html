About Me

    I am a graduate student at Virginia Tech. Prior to joining VT, I worked as a Data scientist, where I was involved in the development of ML-based applications. I have extensively worked on developing analytic applications in PySpark, and have significant experience with the application life-cycle - data extraction, processing, modelling, ML models, build and deploy in ETL pipeline.

    While I have majorly worked on Data science projects in the financial domain, currently I am interested in exploring other domains like social media and climate science. Furthermore, I am highly interested in Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing, and also wish to explore multimodal learning and problems at the intersection of NLP and vision.
